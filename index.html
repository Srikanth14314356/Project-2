Storybook Character List

Jenna Ortega

Jenna Marie Ortega is an American actress. She began her career as a child and received recognition for her role as a younger version of Jane in Jane the Virgin. She won an Imagen Award for her role as Harley Diaz in the Disney Channel series Stuck in the Middle.

Ajith Kumar

Ajith Kumar Subramaniam is an Indian actor who works predominantly in Tamil cinema. He has starred in over 63 films, winning multiple awards including Vijay Awards, Cinema Express Awards, and Filmfare Awards South.

Johnny Depp

John Christopher Depp II is an American actor and musician. He has won a Golden Globe and a Screen Actors Guild Award, and received nominations for three Academy Awards and two BAFTAs.
